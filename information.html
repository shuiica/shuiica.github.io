About

Nela Šuica is a graphic designer from Belgrade, specializing in creating layouts for books and catalogs. Graduated from the Univeristy of Arts in Belgrade, she is currently working on her master’s thesis. She leaves her creative mark on numerous projects, particularly in collaboration with Serbian artists. Her passion for art is also reflected in her involvement in the creation of art books for exhibitions.

Nela is also active in education and collective work, as demonstrated by her participation in designing posters for an exhibition alongside students from the Faculty of Applied Arts. Her ability to blend aesthetic and functional aspects of design makes her an indispensable member of any creative team.

Besides her professional work, she enjoys drawing and exploring new techniques and styles, which contributes to her continuous improvement and innovative approach to design.
