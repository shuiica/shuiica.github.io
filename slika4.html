Memento

Project from the Packaging Design course. Every student had to pick a film of their choice and present the narrative and story through the design of a glass bottle. My choice was the 2000 film “Memento” which follows the story of a man suf- ferring from anterograde amnesia. The entire timeline and narrative of the film is presented through his viewpoint, making it out-of-sequence, leaving the audiance dazed and confused as much as the lead character.
