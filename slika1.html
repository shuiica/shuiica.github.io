Black sun

Art book following Marija Ćalić's 2023. photography exhibiton of the same name in the gallery of Ilija M. Kolarac. The subject of the exhibition is based on the lost footage from a private collection of a family and their travel in Africa during the 1960s. My mother and I found a collection of old Ektachrome slides at a flea market. The exact year, names and origin of the found slides is unknown. Using and observing the scanned photographs, I got the idea to create a form of a “fictional diary”, followed by narrative presented as fictional diary entries and present it as a part of the exhibiton.
