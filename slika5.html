FIST festival

Poster design for the 18th annual student theatre festival “FIST”. The slogan for the year 2023, "Someone I (don't) know”, was dealing with the questioning of the concept of identity and its diversity. In constant questioning of the oneself “Who am I?”, we understand that we cannot separate our personality from every- thing that surrounds us and we can see how that directly impacts the perception of our own identity.
