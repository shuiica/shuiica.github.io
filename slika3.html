Reč i misao

Reč i misao – engl."Word and Thought" book publications were founded in 1959 as the first Yugoslav pocketbook series and quickly gained popularity among readers and critics. Over fifty years of its existence, until 2009, 589 titles by domestic and foreign authors were published, including 100 reprints, with a total circulation of over two and a half million copies. As one of our projects at university we did a redesign of the edition.
