Foggy landscapes

Poster design for the exhibition dedicated toward the life and work of the famous actor Velimir “Bata” Živojinović, held in the Yugoslav film archive. I created posters of the 1984 film "Foggy Landscapes" (Pejzaži u magli ), starring Bata Živoji- nović, Anamarija Petričević and Ljubomir Todorović.
