Kafe Skica

Visual identity for the coffee shop "Skica" (English translation - Sketch) located in the old town neighbourhood, Kosančićev venac, in Belgrade. The project consists of a logo and menu redesign, alongside designs for t-shirts, cups, bags etc. I took the inspiration for the logo from the chairs placed in front of the coffee shop, overlooking the river Sava. Drawing various sketches of the same motive, i was able to group them and create multiple variations of the same logo.
